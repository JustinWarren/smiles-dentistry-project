About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1994 by Dr. Bob Vance, Smiles Dentistry has always had a "customer-first" approach. After years of serving the Charlotte community out of one location, Smiles Dentistry expanded to a second location in 2018.

We are full-service dental office an we specialize in a number of different services including:

  • Braces
  • Cleanings
  • Root Canals
  • Cosmetic Dentistry

"Quality service with for your smile.""
